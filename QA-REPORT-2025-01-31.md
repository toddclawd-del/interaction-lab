## Issues Found

### P1 (High) - None

### P2 (Medium)

| # | Issue | Location | Recommendation |
|---|-------|----------|----------------|
| 1 | **Missing explicit input labels** | Form Inputs section | Add `<label>` elements or `aria-label` to inputs. Placeholders alone don't meet WCAG 3.3.2 |
| 2 | **Focus ring visibility** | All interactive elements | Verify focus rings are visible at 4.5:1 contrast. Consider `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-white/50` |

### P3 (Low)

| # | Issue | Location | Recommendation |
|---|-------|----------|----------------|
| 3 | **Missing favicon** | Site-wide | Add favicon.ico to public folder |
| 4 | **Code blocks lack syntax highlighting** | "View Code" panels | Consider Prism.js or Shiki for JSX highlighting |
| 5 | **Mobile tabs overflow indicator** | `MobileTabs` | Add gradient fade on edges to indicate scroll |
